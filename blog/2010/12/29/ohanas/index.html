Ohana's

Dec 29th, 2010

Spam musubi!

Ohana in Belltown is “a Japanese-Polynesian grill and bamboo sushi lounge” and has been the place for a lot of my friends’ 21sts, usually to good effect (though I guess the waitress during my 21st was a little rude? I was too busy having fun to notice…mostly). I’ve never been there outside of happy hour.

My favorite two types of sushi at the fore <3
And Andrea’s unagi in the background, which I can’t eat because my family curse

I don’t usually have much of a discerning taste when it comes to sushi (since I dislike the kinds with non-salmon fish, which rules out the majority of sushi), but the California rolls here are somehow way more delicious than anywhere else I’ve tried them.  The spam musubi are also super huge, and the gyoza is good too.

omgggg you know you want this

I’ve tasted some sort of really tasty drink that they serve in a white plastic bucket – something very tropical and sweet and pineapple-y – but I don’t know what it’s called, and every time I try to order it the waiters never have any idea what I’m talking about, so it’s probably some mysterious special.  The Mala Shake (which is actually on the menu) suffices after failed attempts.

This will be the last time you ever see it
