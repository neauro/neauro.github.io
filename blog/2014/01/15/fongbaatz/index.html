Website: Fong Baatz Portfolio

Jan 15th, 2014

screenshot of fong baatz portfolio

For some weeks now I’ve been working on the portfolio site of a local artist (who is also A’s mother). It’s been fun. The website can be accessed here. Parts of the process are documented on my Tumblr.

This weekend she and other artists are having an open house at Magnuson Building 30, which was repurposed by the city of Seattle for their work. The open house is on January 19th, from 2:00 to 5:00 pm. Check it out!
