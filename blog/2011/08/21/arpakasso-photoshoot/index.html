Arpakasso Photoshoot

Aug 21st, 2011

This morning Kelly and Chris and I grabbed breakfast at Boogaloo’s in Valencia (which is delicious…and oh man, have I not actually eaten since then?! It’s filling too!). Then Kelly showed us his monome in his apartment, and I discovered his stash of Arpakasso. I ask if I could take them away for pictures before he redistributes them to his sisters, he says yes, I frolic away and spend the rest of the day taking endearing shots.


One thing that I love about Arpakasso is the personality of each Arpakasso’s face. It’s the kind of personality that can only come from being a low-budget toy that is reproduced messily in the thousands, that costs less than a dollar to make, and ends up selling for $2 to an $infinite amount since it can only be obtained in crane games.

and still you want them so bad. so bad

I placed the arupaka on a white desk before a (mostly) white wall and alternated between my 35mm and my 55-200mm, which I zoomed in close from several feet away to get little details that the prime couldn’t focus on. And in general I had a ton of fun taking silly pictures, all of which you can see here.

楽しんでね！
