Sakura-Con 2011 Cosplay Photos: Eeveelutions!

Nov 29th, 2011

eevee-lution cosplay!

Finally received some of our edited photos from the Eeveelution cosplay that we the merchandising staff did for Sakura-Con 2011! They are all located on the photographer’s site here. There are also some group shots that a friend Rachel took on her Flickr stream, starting here.

rawr! (source)

A couple weeks back we finally decided on the theme for our group cosplay for Sakura-Con 2012.  It is…Okami!  We have representatives for all of the Celestial Brush Gods.  I am going to be Moegami, the Phoenix, and it’s likely I’m going to be wearing a kimono-like thing yet again.

but it’s gonna be all about the skirt next year!
