The weather’s getting warm!  In my sunlit wanderings, I spotted a squirrel in a bloom-stripped cherry tree…

trying desperately not to be seen

…a sunbathing turtle…



not trying not to be seen, but succeeding

…a treed Andou…

don’t care nothin’

…and some of the last cherry blossoms.



They’re pretty beautiful.

now get out of here so I can live my life again
