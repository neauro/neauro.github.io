I Graduated From College!

Jun 13th, 2011

de, me, andou, brother, nanay

Now I have a degree in Computer Science!

The ceremony was pretty legit. There were a couple good-natured and amusing name mistakes, and a lot of o___o faces when the titles of the PHD students’ theses were presented. The reception afterward included a couple very delicious cakes, despite what may have been thought about the existence of those cakes previously.

Afterward, we went to The Rusty Pelican for MORE FOOD.

blackberry milkshake!

san francisco melt!

strawberry and banana crepes!

All these things were good but despite my wish to adventure into different realms of food, my favorites at the Rusty Pelican are still the California Scrambler and Pistachio Cake.
