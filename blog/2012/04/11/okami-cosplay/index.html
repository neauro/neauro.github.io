Okami Cosplay

Apr 11th, 2012

The roosterphoenix god of Okami is almost always seen indulging in some smoking pipe.  It’s so characteristic that I knew I had to make for this year’s planned cosplay.

moegami and pipe (source)

I’d never made a cosplay prop before, and had resigned myself to finding a thick dowel of some kind to paint, when I rounded an aisle and discovered…plastic candles.


behold, a plastic candle

They were perfect.  I purchased a pair of them.  They were so cheap that I could unscrew the individual components apart, after which I used a hot glue gun to attach two of the plastic candle bodies to each other.

plastic candle, dissembled

After painting and some more hot glue gun work, the thing was complete!

too lazy to get a non-f/1.8 shot of this

As for how the rest of the cosplay/cosplay group turned out, here’s a photo taken by Photography by Coolpplse near our Merchandising booth!
