March Food & Local Travels

Mar 28th, 2012

En route Cutting Board, one of my favorite sushi places, Andou and I got lost. In the evening the gray polygons framing the highway near west Seattle are especially loomy. Husks of globalism!


glimmery west seattle

We circled about and found the sushi place again, as well as the cause of our lostness: the road we used to take is rubble now.  I don’t have a picture of it, but Saga is the best roll at Cutting Board – deep-fried salmon goodness.
