Finally, WWF

Aug 6th, 2011


Happily playing Words with Friends after having always been excluded from it due to my lack of smartphone.

It’s not really going that well for me…;;

Some thoughts:

  • I wish the “Swap” button would tell you that you’d sacrifice your turn in order to get new tiles.
  • I also wish those buttons were different colors/higher contrast and that the “Pass” option was red or something, but maybe there’s a strategy to passing that I don’t understand yet.
  • The sound effects are cute, and I like the feed on the left side of the screen (not pictured here, I suppose) which tells you what word your opponent played last.  This is maybe an artifact of the iPhone game?

In other news, it’s fun to have a game that I can play even with my lesser-known relatives. :D

even if they are also super good;;
