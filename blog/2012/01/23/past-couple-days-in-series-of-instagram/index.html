The Past Couple Days in a Series of Instagram Pictures

Jan 23rd, 2012

bainbridge island

I took the ferry toward the first stirrings of Seattle Snowpacalypse 2012.

snowy pines outside andou’s

The first snow was not enough to keep most people from work, but it eventually piled up steep and (once it melted, then froze) slippery.  The sidewalk curbs downtown were framed with gray-brown heaps of gravely snow which at first glance seemed hard-packed by passerby but upon traversal collapsed a stepper’s foot into surprise murky puddles ankle-deep.

I made it into work on Friday, but the weekend came soon enough, and with it another shot at Arkham Horror.  This time we had two newbies, and to everyone’s shock (and delight), we didn’t close up all the gates before the Ancient One came.

good ol’ glaaki

We took turns hammering away at it, and might have actually still won had the Doom Counter not advanced a couple tokens due to some unfortunate Mythos events.  This time I played the Nun and I have to say: having no money is not that great when all the gates open up beyond the train station, and you need a dollar to get there.  I also had a hard time finding clue tokens, and stayed at The Witch House and The Unnamable for most of the game, gathering Allies in frustration (just give me clue tokens!).

The weekend ended with me getting a good start on this year’s Okami cosplay, and then visiting Andou’s house to assemble and subsequently devour some dozens of delicious jyaozi for Chinese New Year.

新年快乐!
