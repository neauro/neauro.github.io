Overview for
Dream Builder & Elements

Dream Builder: Amusement Park is a casual simulation PC game where players create an amusement park. As the game's designer, I led a team of one programmer and four artists. I also programmed UI and UI animations, and did project management, including overseeing general scheduling and in-house playtests.
