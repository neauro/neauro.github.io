Hey! I like building things that make people happy.

  • Introducing a user-centered design process for a brand-new (and still under-wraps) music streaming app,
  • Heading up front-end development at Reveal Chat,
  • Game design and UI animations for Dream Builder: Amusement Park,
  • short stories Iron & Honey and Monsterfolk for UW's speculative fiction journal AU,
  • and a cosplay of Strength from Black Rock Shooter.
