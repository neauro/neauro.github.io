Alex(ander) and Panda(monium)

Aug 17th, 2012


facebook’s well-intentioned computer vision

Yesterday I went to a friend’s housewarming party. There were cats!

Maybe it was actually a catwarming party?

welcome to my carpet castle

 There are two four-month-old cats. The white doll-faced persian is named Alexander, or Alex for short.

who goes there

Alex is the more peaceful of the two, and likes being held.

#catlyfe

Pandamonium, or Panda, is a himalayan, the beloved breed of my friend’s boyfriend’s family line. True to name, he seems to be more of an adventurous go-getter.

panda & mouse

all those cat toys, and he decides to play on law textbooks
with the window puller thing

Both cats (as well as us) were affected by the high heat we’ve had recently, and were seeking various cool spots around the apartment.

catwarming indeed

hot naptime

The shelf in front of the fan seemed to be the preferred location, secondary only to the fridge, into which Panda snuck into at every opportunity, lying down on the bottom shelf by the produce.

I guess I would be hot too if I were a long-haired cat, though apparently their soft outer coat is “thin” right now for the summer.

an outer coat blowing in the wind

Later, we grilled kabobs on the roof and dined merrily beside gorgeous scenery.

goodnight, downtown!
