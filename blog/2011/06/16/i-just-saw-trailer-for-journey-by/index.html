I Just Saw a Trailer for Journey by Thatgamecompany!

Jun 16th, 2011


“One thing that we want to try for Journey is to create this feeling between two complete strangers…so it’s like, you’re on a hike and you’re heading somewhere, and on the pass you might run across other players pass, and you guys could stay together, or you could leave each other.”

“One of the ideas of Journey was to create a space in which you could feel a sense of maybe awe or wonder. So Journey presents a very different experience in which you are left kind of powerless against the environment you’re presented with.”


I haven’t felt so much hope and excitement for a game in such a long time, much less wanted to purchase a PS3 just to play something. I’m so happy that people still make console games that aren’t just army gamez.

(source)

It occurs to me now that I’ve always been more of a console gamer, but in the past couple years have played games mostly on the PC, most of them either older releases like Bioshock and Portal, or Flash games. When I was younger games like Sonic the Hedgehog or Castle of Illusion Starring Mickey Mouse were fine, but nowadays I really crave story, for a game to make me think something new. Games are too expensive to risk going beyond gunsight and realistic blood nowadays, I suppose.
